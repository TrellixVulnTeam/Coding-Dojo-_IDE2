My Review of Operation Tango for Playstation 5

Operation Tango

Operation Tango is a spy game in which players (you and a friend) play through a series of missions. One player is a spy, one player is a hacker. Players cannot see each others screens, which makes this game very interesting and frustrating. The frustrating portions are do to explaining what you need done by your accomplice or vice versus, while performing actions on your side, for example: describing in detail a picture or sequence of number to your friend, so that they can perform some action on their screen, so that you can continue on your screen. All in all good game, but needs lots of patience.
